Band Bio

The Niche have been entertaining Chicago area audiences for over 20 years. The Niche will always bring in a great crowd and put on a great show! Their eclectic mix of 60's and 70's hits, with a few "B" sides thown in, is guaranteed to keep the crowd dancing and listening throughout the night. The Niche is flexible to meet the demands of the gig. They are available in 4, 3, 2 piece and solo configurations.
